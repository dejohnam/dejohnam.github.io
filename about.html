about | Amber DeJohn

Originally from the Flint-area in Michigan, I'm currently an Assistant Professor at Florida State University. I am cross-appointed in the Georgaphy department and the Public Health program. Using quantiative, spatial approaches and place-based methods, I mainly study how older adults access health-promoting destinations and socializing opportunities. See some of my maps.

I am currently conducting a study on older adults in Florida's captial region. If you or someone you know is 55 or older and lives in the greater-Tallahassee region, take a peek at the opportunities on the Ongoing Studies tab.
